Apex Industrial Components reported revenue of INR 960 crore for FY2024-25 and improved EBITDA margins.
Total debt stood at INR 290 crore, while total liabilities were reported at INR 540 crore.
Cash flow from operations for the year was INR 125 crore with a stable working capital cycle.
The company disclosed two ongoing litigation matters related to contractual disputes with suppliers.
The board also noted a related party transaction policy update and resignation of one independent director.
Management commentary highlighted no material fraud observations and ongoing governance strengthening.
